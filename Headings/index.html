Explore the World of Space

Learn about planets, stars, galaxies, and cosmic phenomena.

The Solar System

The Solar System consists of the Sun and all celestial objects bound to it by gravity, including planets, moons, asteroids, and comets.

Planets

There are eight planets in the Solar System, each with unique characteristics and orbits around the Sun.

Inner Planets

Mercury, Venus, Earth, and Mars are called inner planets because they lie closer to the Sun.

Outer Planets

Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune are outer planets, mostly gas giants, far from the Sun.

Asteroids and Comets

Asteroids are rocky objects, while comets are icy bodies that develop tails when approaching the Sun.

Galaxies

Beyond our Solar System, billions of galaxies exist in the universe, each containing billions of stars and planets.
